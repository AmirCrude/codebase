Clearance Management System

Hawassa University Streamline your end-of-year clearance process. Submit requests, track status across 9 verifying officers, and get cleared all in one place.

Get Started

How It Works

📝

1. Submit Request

Initiate your clearance request online in seconds no more standing in long queues.

👥

2. 9 Verifying Officers

Department Head, Faculty Dean, Dormitory Chief, Library Chief, Bookstore Keeper, Student Service, Sports Master, FAR, and Data Analyst all connected digitally.

📊

3. Real-Time Tracking

Monitor your clearance progress across all units in real time. Get notified when each officer approves.

✅

4. Get Cleared

Once all 8 officers approve and the Faculty Assistant Registrar gives final approval, download your clearance certificate.
